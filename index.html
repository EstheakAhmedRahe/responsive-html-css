About US

The market size for the restaurant industry in Bangladesh has been growing for quite a few years now. It was not too long ago when there were very limited options and people would have a hard time to find a decent budget friendly place to socialize with others over some quality food. The days of despair are now over and there is ever growing love for the dining out culture. There was a prominent gap in the restaurant industry which was explored and experimented with by only a handful number of restaurants. Over time, it has turned out to be one of the industries with the highest potential for high profit margins.
